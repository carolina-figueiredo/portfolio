Fanzine.

Monsters, Saul Bass.

For this project, my team and I created a distinctive fanzine titled Monsters, an avant-garde magazine that we made at the Professional Exploration Week and my university lade. This fanzine pays tribute to visionary designers - the true "monsters" of creativity. Inspired by the transformative work of these artists, we aimed to capture the extraordinary spirit and talent of our "Monster", Saul Bass. After researching his amazing work we designed the fanzine in Illustrator proceeded by screen printing the fanzine using just three colors, yellow purple and black.
